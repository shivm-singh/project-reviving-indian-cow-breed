About Indigenous Cow Breeds

India is home to some of the most diverse and resilient indigenous cow breeds in the world. These breeds have evolved over centuries to thrive in local conditions and play a vital role in sustainable agriculture.

However, many of these native breeds are now endangered due to the increasing popularity of exotic breeds and changing agricultural practices.

At Gaumitra, we're committed to preserving these genetic treasures through conservation efforts, research, and community engagement.

Smart Breeding & Indian Cow Revival

Reviving native Indian cow breeds through smart breeding programs is crucial for sustainable dairy farming, biodiversity, and organic farming. Indigenous breeds like **Gir, Sahiwal, Red Sindhi, Tharparkar, and Kankrej** are resilient, produce high-quality milk, and support ecological balance.
